Breakfast:

Scrambled eggs with spinach, tomatoes, and feta cheese.
Whole grain toast with avocado slices or butter.
A glass of milk or a cup of Greek yogurt.
Mid-Morning Snack:

Sliced turkey or chicken breast with whole grain crackers.
A small handful of mixed nuts (such as almonds, walnuts, and cashews).
Lunch:

Grilled salmon or tuna salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and olives.
Quinoa or brown rice pilaf with roasted vegetables (such as carrots, zucchini, and bell peppers).
Afternoon Snack:

Cottage cheese with sliced peaches or pineapple.
A hard-boiled egg or protein bar.
Dinner:

Grilled chicken or turkey breast with sweet potato mash and steamed broccoli.
Baked fish fillet (such as cod or tilapia) with asparagus and quinoa.
Evening Snack (Optional):

Greek yogurt parfait with mixed berries and granola.
Sliced apple or pear with cheese slices (such as cheddar or Swiss).
Hydration:

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated.
Include herbal teas or infused water for variety.
Notes:

Choose lean protein sources such as poultry, fish, eggs, and low-fat dairy products to support muscle growth and repair.
Incorporate a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats into your meals for optimal nutrition.
Monitor portion sizes and listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues.
Consider consulting with a registered dietitian or nutritionist for personalized dietary advice and guidance on maintaining a healthy body.
